Standing By YOU Every Step of the Way

With 30 years experience in Real Estate Industry, you can feel confident that all your real estate needswill be looked after, and I will be there Standing By YOU Every Step Of The Way! Starting my career in 1988, as a receptionist, working my way through as a deal secretary, administrator and then Executive Office administrator for a large downtown ROyal Lepage Brokerage in Torronto, allowed me to spend time with my children growing up, participating in their schooling, and their organized sport events.

In 2007, with the support and encouragement of Royal LePage Estate Service Inc., it was time to broaden my knowledge, and take the real estate course with Ontairo Real Estate Association (OREA) to obtain my Real Estate Licence in Ontairo.
